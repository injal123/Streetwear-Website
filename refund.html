Refund Policy for StreetSync

At StreetSync, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. If you find that our products do not meet your expectations, we have a straightforward refund policy in place to make the process as smooth as possible.

Eligibility:

Refunds are available for items that are damaged upon arrival, defective, or if you receive the wrong item.

To be eligible for a refund, please initiate the request within 14 days of receiving your order.

Conditions:

Products must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging.

Refunds are not applicable for items on sale or marked as final sale.

Process:

Contact our customer support team at anzel1@gmail.com , within 14 days of receiving your order.

Provide order details, a clear photo of the product in question, and a brief explanation of the issue. Our team will review your request and provide further instructions on the return process.

Refund Method:

Refunds will be issued through the original payment method or an alternative agreed upon with the customer.

Exceptions:

We do not offer refunds for change of mind or if the product does not fit as expected. Please refer to our sizing guide before making a purchase.

By shopping with StreetSync, you agree to and understand our refund policy. We are committed to ensuring you have a positive experience with our streetwear products.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our customer support team at anzel1@gmail.com .

Thank you for choosing StreetSync!
